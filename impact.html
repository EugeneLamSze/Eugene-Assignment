Impact of Famous Scientists

James Watson

The discovery of the helical structure of DNA explains the mechanism of DNA replication. It revolutionized the study of biology and advanced biological techniques with the invention of recombinant DNA technologies. Watson’s lead in Human Genome Project provides the molecular basis of cancer gene, allowing scientists to understand the cause of cancer and work on the treatment of the disease.

Frederick Banting

Banting transformed the treatment of diabetes from conservative strict diet treatment to revolutionary insulin injection and extend the lifespan of diabetic patients. Banting invention of G-suit brings helps human to overcome the challenges in G force in the aviation and aerospace industry.

Charles Kao Kuen

Kao developed important techniques and configurations for glass fibre waveguides. In telecommunication, fiber optic technology has replaced copper wire in long-distance telephone lines, and it is used to link computers within local area networks. In medicinal application, fiber optics is used as the basis of the fiberscopes for endoscopy. Kao’s revolutionary work laid the present trans-oceanic fibre-optic cable that made a major impact in our daily lives
